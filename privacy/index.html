GlucoBeacon

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your health data never leaves your device.

Your Data Stays on Your Device

GlucoBeacon is a local-first app. All blood sugar readings, lifestyle logs, settings, and health data are stored exclusively on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. We do not operate servers that store your health data, and your readings are never transmitted to us or any third party.

What We Collect

We do not collect personal health data. The only data processed outside your device is anonymized purchase receipts verified by RevenueCat solely to validate your Pro subscription status. No health information is included in this process. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.

In-App Purchases

GlucoBeacon Pro subscriptions are processed securely by Apple App Store or Google Play. Purchase validation is handled by RevenueCat, which receives only your subscription receipt — not your health data. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Health Platform Integrations

If you choose to connect Apple Health or Google Health Connect, blood sugar readings you log in GlucoBeacon may be written to those platforms. This integration is entirely optional, operates on-device, and is governed by Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies. GlucoBeacon does not read any data from these platforms that you have not explicitly authorized.

Notifications

Reminder and trend alert notifications are scheduled and delivered locally on your device. Notification content is never transmitted off your device and is not accessible to us.

Analytics & Crash Reporting

GlucoBeacon does not use any third-party analytics or crash reporting SDKs. No usage data, screen views, or behavioral events are collected or transmitted.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings → Data → Clear All Data. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all locally stored data from your device. To delete your RevenueCat purchase record, contact us at support@glucobeacon.app.

Children's Privacy

GlucoBeacon is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us and we will assist with data removal.

Medical Disclaimer

GlucoBeacon is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. A1C estimates, trend alerts, and pattern insights are informational only and are not a substitute for professional medical judgment. Always consult a licensed healthcare provider before making changes to your diabetes management plan.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at support@glucobeacon.app.
