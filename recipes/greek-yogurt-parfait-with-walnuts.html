Greek Yogurt Parfait with Walnuts

5 min total · 1 servings

Creamy Greek yogurt layered with blueberries, crunchy walnuts, and cinnamon.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 22g of carbs per serving, it's worth pairing with protein or fat and watching portion size to keep the resulting blood sugar rise gradual, and its estimated glycemic index of ~25 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 20g of protein per serving also helps slow digestion and blunt any post-meal rise.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 2 tbsp walnuts
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Stir vanilla and cinnamon into Greek yogurt.
  2. Layer yogurt, blueberries, and walnuts in a glass or bowl.
  3. Serve immediately or refrigerate overnight.
