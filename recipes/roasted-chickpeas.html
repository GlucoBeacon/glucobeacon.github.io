Roasted Chickpeas

40 min total · 2 servings

Crunchy spiced roasted chickpeas — a satisfying chip alternative with fiber and protein.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

With only 18g of carbs per serving, it's a lighter-carb option without needing a strict low-carb framing, and its estimated glycemic index of ~28 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 5g of fiber per serving adds the same slowing effect.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup chickpeas
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Rinse and thoroughly dry chickpeas.
  3. Toss with olive oil and spices.
  4. Spread on a baking sheet.
  5. Roast 30–35 minutes, shaking halfway, until crispy.
