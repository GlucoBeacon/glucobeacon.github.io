Sweet Potato Hash

30 min total · 1 servings

Colorful sweet potato hash with bell peppers and eggs — a satisfying weekend breakfast.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 40g of carbs per serving, it's worth pairing with protein or fat and watching portion size to keep the resulting blood sugar rise gradual, and its estimated glycemic index of ~50 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 6g of fiber per serving adds the same slowing effect.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium sweet potato
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup onion
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Dice sweet potato into small cubes.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet; add sweet potato and cook 10 minutes.
  3. Add bell pepper and onion; season with paprika, garlic powder, and pepper.
  4. Cook 5 more minutes until vegetables are tender.
  5. Make wells in the hash, crack in eggs, cover, and cook 3–4 minutes.
