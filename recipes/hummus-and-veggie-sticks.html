Hummus and Veggie Sticks

5 min total · 1 servings

Creamy hummus paired with a rainbow of raw vegetable sticks for dipping.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 29g of carbs per serving, it's worth pairing with protein or fat and watching portion size to keep the resulting blood sugar rise gradual, and its estimated glycemic index of ~30 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 10g of fiber per serving adds the same slowing effect.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup hummus
  • 1 cup carrots
  • 1 cup celery
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut carrots, celery, and bell pepper into sticks.
  2. Serve with hummus for dipping.
