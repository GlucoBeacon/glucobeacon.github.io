Quinoa Breakfast Bowl

20 min total · 1 servings

Warm quinoa topped with almond butter, banana, and berries for lasting energy.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 77g of carbs per serving, it's worth pairing with protein or fat and watching portion size to keep the resulting blood sugar rise gradual, and its estimated glycemic index of ~40 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 10g of fiber per serving adds the same slowing effect.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup quinoa
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1/2 medium banana
  • 1/4 cup blueberries
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa according to package directions using almond milk instead of water.
  2. Spoon into a bowl and stir in almond butter and cinnamon.
  3. Top with sliced banana and blueberries.
