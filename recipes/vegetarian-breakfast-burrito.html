Vegetarian Breakfast Burrito

20 min total · 1 servings

A hearty scrambled egg, black bean, and cheese burrito wrapped up for a grab-and-go morning.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 42g of carbs per serving, it's worth pairing with protein or fat and watching portion size to keep the resulting blood sugar rise gradual, and its estimated glycemic index of ~45 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 29g of protein per serving also helps slow digestion and blunt any post-meal rise, and 8g of fiber per serving adds the same slowing effect.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup black beans
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup cheddar cheese
  • 1 piece flour tortilla
  • 2 tbsp salsa

Instructions

  1. Scramble eggs with diced bell pepper.
  2. Warm black beans.
  3. Fill tortilla with eggs, beans, and cheese.
  4. Top with salsa, wrap, and serve.
