Apple Slices with Almond Butter

3 min total · 1 servings

Crisp apple slices with creamy natural almond butter — a perfectly balanced snack.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 30g of carbs per serving, it's worth pairing with protein or fat and watching portion size to keep the resulting blood sugar rise gradual, and its estimated glycemic index of ~38 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium apple
  • 2 tbsp almond butter

Instructions

  1. Core and slice apple into wedges.
  2. Serve with almond butter for dipping.
