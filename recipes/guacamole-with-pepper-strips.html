Guacamole with Pepper Strips

10 min total · 2 servings

Fresh homemade guacamole served with crisp bell pepper strips instead of chips.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 12g of carbs per serving, it fits comfortably within most low-carb diabetes meal plans, and its estimated glycemic index of ~15 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium avocado
  • 1 1/2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp cilantro
  • 1/2 clove garlic
  • 1 tbsp red onion
  • 1/2 piece jalapeño
  • 1 medium bell pepper

Instructions

  1. Mash avocado with lime juice and a fork.
  2. Mix in minced garlic, cilantro, red onion, and jalapeño.
  3. Slice bell pepper into strips for dipping.
  4. Serve immediately.
