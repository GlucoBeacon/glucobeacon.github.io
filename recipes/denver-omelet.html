Denver Omelet

20 min total · 1 servings

A classic diner omelet with ham, bell pepper, onion, and melted cheddar.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 8g of carbs per serving, it fits comfortably within most low-carb diabetes meal plans, and its estimated glycemic index of ~10 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 33g of protein per serving also helps slow digestion and blunt any post-meal rise.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 slice ham
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup onion
  • 1/4 cup cheddar cheese
  • 1 tsp butter

Instructions

  1. Dice ham, bell pepper, and onion; sauté in butter 3 minutes.
  2. Whisk eggs and pour over vegetables.
  3. Cook until edges set, then sprinkle cheddar on one half.
  4. Fold omelet over and serve.
