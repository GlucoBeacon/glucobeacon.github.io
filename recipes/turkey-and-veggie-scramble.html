Turkey and Veggie Scramble

15 min total · 1 servings

Hearty morning scramble with lean turkey and colorful vegetables.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 8g of carbs per serving, it fits comfortably within most low-carb diabetes meal plans, and its estimated glycemic index of ~15 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 35g of protein per serving also helps slow digestion and blunt any post-meal rise.

Ingredients

  • 3 oz ground turkey
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup zucchini
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder

Instructions

  1. Cook ground turkey in olive oil over medium heat until no longer pink.
  2. Add bell pepper and zucchini; cook 3 minutes.
  3. Whisk eggs with spices and pour over turkey mixture.
  4. Scramble gently until eggs are just set.
