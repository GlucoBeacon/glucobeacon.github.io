Bacon Egg and Cheese Bites

30 min total · 4 servings

Oven-baked egg muffins loaded with bacon and cheddar — a grab-and-go keto breakfast.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 1g of carbs per serving, it fits comfortably within most low-carb diabetes meal plans, and its estimated glycemic index of ~10 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 16g of protein per serving also helps slow digestion and blunt any post-meal rise.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 4 slice bacon
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp chives
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and grease a muffin tin.
  2. Cook bacon until crisp; crumble.
  3. Whisk eggs with cream, salt, and pepper.
  4. Divide bacon and cheddar among muffin cups; pour egg mixture over.
  5. Bake 18-20 minutes until set.
  6. Top with chives before serving.
