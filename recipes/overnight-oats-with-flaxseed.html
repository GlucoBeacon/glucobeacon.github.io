Overnight Oats with Flaxseed

5 min total · 1 servings

Make-ahead oats packed with omega-3 flaxseed for a filling, blood-sugar-friendly breakfast.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 40g of carbs per serving, it's worth pairing with protein or fat and watching portion size to keep the resulting blood sugar rise gradual, and its estimated glycemic index of ~40 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 7g of fiber per serving adds the same slowing effect.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup blueberries
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Combine oats, flaxseed, almond milk, and yogurt in a jar.
  2. Stir in cinnamon.
  3. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  4. Top with blueberries before serving.
