Avocado and Egg Toast

10 min total · 1 servings

Creamy avocado on whole grain toast topped with a perfectly cooked egg.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 24g of carbs per serving, it's worth pairing with protein or fat and watching portion size to keep the resulting blood sugar rise gradual, and its estimated glycemic index of ~40 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 7g of fiber per serving adds the same slowing effect.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice whole grain bread
  • 1/2 medium avocado
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 0.12 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread until golden.
  2. Mash avocado with lemon juice and black pepper.
  3. Fry or poach egg to your liking.
  4. Spread avocado on toast, top with egg, and sprinkle red pepper flakes.
