Chia Seed Pudding

5 min total · 1 servings

Silky chia pudding made overnight with almond milk and topped with fresh strawberries.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 23g of carbs per serving, it fits comfortably within most low-carb diabetes meal plans, and its estimated glycemic index of ~15 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 15g of fiber per serving adds the same slowing effect.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup strawberries

Instructions

  1. Whisk chia seeds, almond milk, vanilla, and cinnamon together.
  2. Pour into a jar and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  3. Stir well before serving.
  4. Top with sliced strawberries.
