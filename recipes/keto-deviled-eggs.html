Keto Deviled Eggs

15 min total · 2 servings

Classic creamy deviled eggs with a dusting of paprika.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 1g of carbs per serving, it fits comfortably within most low-carb diabetes meal plans, and its estimated glycemic index of ~5 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 13g of protein per serving also helps slow digestion and blunt any post-meal rise.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp yellow mustard
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 0.12 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Hard boil eggs, cool, and peel.
  2. Halve eggs and scoop yolks into a bowl.
  3. Mash yolks with mayonnaise, mustard, and pepper.
  4. Pipe filling back into egg whites; dust with paprika.
