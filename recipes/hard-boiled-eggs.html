Hard Boiled Eggs

13 min total · 1 servings

Simple protein-packed hard-boiled eggs — the ultimate portable, no-spike snack.

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

At 1g of carbs per serving, it fits comfortably within most low-carb diabetes meal plans, and its estimated glycemic index of ~0 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast, and 12g of protein per serving also helps slow digestion and blunt any post-meal rise.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 0.12 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Place eggs in cold water; bring to a boil.
  2. Cover and cook exactly 9 minutes.
  3. Transfer to ice water to stop cooking.
  4. Peel and season with black pepper.
