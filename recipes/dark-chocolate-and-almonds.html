Dark Chocolate and Almonds

Why this fits a diabetes-friendly diet

With only 18g of carbs per serving, it's a lighter-carb option without needing a strict low-carb framing, and its estimated glycemic index of ~25 means the carbs it does have tend to digest slowly rather than spiking blood sugar fast.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz dark chocolate
  • 1 oz almonds

Instructions

  1. Break dark chocolate into small pieces.
  2. Pair with a small handful of raw almonds.
