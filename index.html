Privacy First

Your health data belongs to you.

In an industry where health data is bought and sold, GlucoBeacon is different. Everything stays on your device — always.

📵

No Account Required

Open the app and start logging. No email, no password, no profile to create.

💾

On-Device Storage

Your readings, medications, and logs live in an SQLite database on your phone — never uploaded, never synced to us.

🔒

No Data Selling

We don't collect, analyze, or sell your health information. There is no "us" in your data.

☁️

Optional Backup

If you want backup, connect your own Google Drive. The file goes to your account, not ours.

🛠️

Anonymous Crash Reports

If the app ever crashes, a technical report — device model and error trace, never your health data — helps us fix it. That's the only thing sent automatically.
